Astar Ecosystem

Astar Network hosts a growing ecosystem of DeFi protocols, NFT platforms, infrastructure providers, and developer tooling. The Build2Earn dApp staking mechanism uniquely incentivises ecosystem growth by directing ASTR token inflation to developers of registered dApps, creating a self-sustaining developer economy on Polkadot.

DeFi Protocols

Decentralised Exchanges

Multiple AMM-based DEXs operate on Astar's EVM layer, providing token swaps, liquidity pools, and yield farming. These protocols leverage Astar's low transaction costs (~6s blocks) for efficient trading without Ethereum gas price volatility.

Lending & Borrowing

Money market protocols on Astar enable collateralised borrowing of ASTR and bridged assets. Cross-chain collateral via XCM is an emerging use case, allowing DOT and KSM holders to access Astar DeFi without token bridging.

dApp Staking Participants

Any deployed smart contract can be registered in the Build2Earn dApp staking registry. Registered dApps attract ASTR stakers, and developer teams receive era-based rewards proportional to staked amounts, creating a passive funding stream.

Wallets

EVM Wallets

MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, and any EIP-1193-compatible wallet can connect to Astar by adding the network (Chain ID 592, RPC: your Dwellir endpoint). Hardware wallets (Ledger, Trezor) work via MetaMask's hardware wallet integration.

Substrate Wallets

Polkadot.js browser extension and Talisman wallet support native Substrate accounts (SS58 address format). These wallets enable dApp staking, governance voting, and XCM transfers using the native ASTR token representation.

Unified Wallets

SubWallet and Nova Wallet provide unified interfaces for both EVM and Substrate accounts, supporting ASTR transfers, staking, and NFT management across both address spaces from a single app.

Developer Community

Astar has an active developer community with official channels for technical support and collaboration. The Astar Forum, Discord, and developer portal provide resources for building on both EVM and WASM tracks. Astar hosts regular hackathons and the Astar Builders Program for teams seeking ecosystem grants and mentorship.
